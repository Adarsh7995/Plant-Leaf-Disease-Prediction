Treatment :

1.Pruning the trees in spring and pollinating them in early summer or even in late spring.It will help to prevent the trees from growing leaves in the fall,a period that insects are most active.
2.Another option is to water the trees.keeping the trees well-watered helps prevent infections. Insects like aphids are more active in dry periods and areas of drought.
3.Treating the tree after harvest is good practice too.the tree can be treated with some special chemicals or oils after the fruits have been harvested to help it maintain its healthy state.
4.Acceleration of the decomposition and/or destruction of the end of season fallen leaf pile should be prioritised,especially before heavy rains,as to limit the potential spread of ascospores.
